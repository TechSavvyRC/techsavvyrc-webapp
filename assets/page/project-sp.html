Description

The observability and monitoring project for Saudi Payments infrastructure aims to ensure operational efficiency by offering insights into the performance and availability of diverse payment methods. It encompasses deploying monitoring tools, custom dashboards, and alerts, along with stakeholder training. Additionally, it ensures regulatory compliance and proactively identifies and resolves issues to maintain uninterrupted service for customers.
